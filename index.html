Hello there! Welcome to my IISER Kolkata webpage, I completed my 5 year BS-MS Dual-degree program at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Kolkata in 2019. During my stay at IISER I was a part of the National Center for High Pressure Studies(NCHPS) as a BS-MS project student under Prof. Goutam Dev Mukherjee, working on developing set-up for low temperature Raman spectroscopy and studying different Rare earth systems, I have also been involved with some computation related work during my stay here. I am also an avid follower of technology, a nature enthusiast, a football fanatic and I love to cook.
